Renzo Sy

My name is Renzo Sy. My friends call me Renzo for short. I am an up and coming Filipino indie Bedroom Pop artist born and raised here in Cebu, Philippines. I’ve been in love with music ever since I was a little kid and learned how to play the guitar at the age of 12, but I’ve only started producing and writing my own songs fairly recently, just around 3 years ago.

I like a lot of genres in music. I listened to a lot of genres growing up, from punk rock to indie folk and even the blues, I’ve enjoyed them all. But these past couple of years, I’ve been listening to a lot of indie music in particular. Something about the woozy dreamy sounds of Boy Pablo, along with the lo-fi production aesthetic from Clairo and topped off with amazing lyrics from the likes of Rex Orange, makes me feel very… at home.

Since I'm relatively brand new to the scene, I'm doing as much as I can to try and connect with as many people in the industry as possible so that I can have a chance to be heard. With that being said, I’m hoping to gain some traction with my most recent EP entitled “Perfectly”, which includes my previous releases and a few new ones as well. I really feel like I’m where I want to be right now in terms of how my music sounds and feels and I want to share this vision with more people. As cliché as it sounds, I write from the heart and if I can connect to more people in some way, shape or form through my music, then I’ll be able to rest easy.
